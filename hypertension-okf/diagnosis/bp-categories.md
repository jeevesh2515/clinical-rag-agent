# BP Categories

## ACC/AHA 2017 Classification

| Category | Systolic (mmHg) | Diastolic (mmHg) | Action |
|----------|----------------|-----------------|--------|
| Normal | <120 | <80 | Recheck in 1 year |
| Elevated | 120–129 | <80 | Lifestyle modification, recheck 3–6 months |
| Stage 1 HTN | 130–139 | 80–89 | Lifestyle + assess ASCVD risk; drug if risk ≥10% |
| Stage 2 HTN | ≥140 | ≥90 | Lifestyle + drug therapy |
| Hypertensive Crisis | ≥180 | and/or ≥120 | Immediate evaluation; may need hospitalization |

## ESC/ESH 2023 Classification

| Category | Systolic (mmHg) | Diastolic (mmHg) |
|----------|----------------|-----------------|
| Optimal | <120 | <80 |
| Normal | 120–129 | 80–84 |
| High Normal | 130–139 | 85–89 |
| Grade 1 HTN | 140–159 | 90–99 |
| Grade 2 HTN | 160–179 | 100–109 |
| Grade 3 HTN | ≥180 | ≥110 |
| Isolated Systolic HTN | ≥140 | <90 |

## NICE (UK) Classification

| Category | Clinic BP |
|----------|-----------|
| Stage 1 HTN | ≥140/90 (confirmed by ABPM/HBPM ≥135/85) |
| Stage 2 HTN | ≥160/100 (confirmed by ABPM/HBPM ≥150/95) |
| Severe HTN | ≥180/110 |
