# Diagnostic Thresholds and Confirmation Criteria

## Diagnosis Confirmation

### Clinic BP Measurement Protocol
- Patient seated for ≥5 minutes, arm supported at heart level
- Use appropriate cuff size (bladder encircles ≥80% of arm)
- Take 3 readings, 1–2 minutes apart; use average of last 2
- Confirm elevated reading on at least 2 separate visits

### Confirmation with ABPM or HBPM
- If clinic BP is 140/90–179/109 (Stage 1 or borderline):
  - Confirm with **ABPM** (24-hour ambulatory): average daytime ≥135/85
  - Or **HBPM** (home): average ≥135/85 over 7 days (discard day 1)
- If clinic BP is ≥180/110 (severe): start treatment immediately, no ABPM needed

### White Coat vs Masked Hypertension

| Condition | Clinic BP | Ambulatory/Home BP |
|-----------|-----------|-------------------|
| Sustained HTN | Elevated | Elevated |
| White Coat HTN | Elevated | Normal |
| Masked HTN | Normal | Elevated |
