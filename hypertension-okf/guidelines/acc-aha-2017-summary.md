# ACC/AHA 2017 Summary

## BP Categories (Revised)

| Category | Systolic | Diastolic |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Normal | <120 | <80 |
| Elevated | 120–129 | <80 |
| Stage 1 HTN | 130–139 | 80–89 |
| Stage 2 HTN | ≥140 | ≥90 |
| Hypertensive Crisis | ≥180 | and/or ≥120 |

## Treatment Thresholds

| Population | Threshold for Drug Therapy |
|------------|---------------------------|
| Clinical CVD or 10-year ASCVD risk ≥10% | ≥130/80 |
| No CVD and ASCVD risk <10% | ≥140/90 |
| CKD or diabetes | ≥130/80 |

## BP Goals

| Population | Goal |
|------------|------|
| General | <130/80 |
| Elderly (≥65, ambulatory) | <130/80 (individualize) |
| CKD | <130/80 |
| Diabetes | <130/80 |

## First-Line Agents

- Thiazide diuretics
- ACE inhibitors
- ARBs
- CCBs
