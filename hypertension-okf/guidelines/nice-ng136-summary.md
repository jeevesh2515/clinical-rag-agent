# NICE NG136 Summary

## BP Classification

| Category | Clinic BP |
|----------|-----------|
| Stage 1 | ≥140/90 (if ABPM/HBPM daytime avg ≥135/85) |
| Stage 2 | ≥160/100 (if ABPM/HBPM daytime avg ≥150/95) |
| Severe HTN | ≥180/110 |

## Treatment Thresholds

| Age/Comorbidity | Threshold |
|-----------------|-----------|
| <80 years, no CKD/diabetes | Stage 1 if target organ damage, CVD, renal disease, diabetes, or 10-yr CVD risk ≥10% |
| <80 years, Stage 2 | Offer treatment regardless of risk |
| ≥80 years | Stage 2 or higher |
| CKD (all ages) | ≥140/90 clinic |

## BP Goals

| Population | Goal |
|------------|------|
| <80 years | <140/90 (clinic) or <135/85 (ABPM/HBPM) |
| ≥80 years | <150/90 (clinic) |

## Stepped Treatment (for adults <80)

| Step | Agent |
|------|-------|
| 1 | ACEi or ARB (if <55, non-black); CCB (if ≥55 or black) |
| 2 | ACEi/ARB + CCB |
| 3 | ACEi/ARB + CCB + thiazide-like diuretic |
| 4 | Add spironolactone (if K+ ≤4.5) or alpha-blockers/beta-blockers |
