# Calcium Channel Blockers

## Dihydropyridine (DHP) CCBs

| Drug | Starting Dose | Max Dose | Frequency |
|------|--------------|----------|-----------|
| Amlodipine | 5 mg | 10 mg | Daily |
| Nifedipine LA | 30 mg | 90 mg | Daily |
| Felodipine | 5 mg | 10 mg | Daily |

## Non-Dihydropyridine CCBs

| Drug | Starting Dose | Max Dose | Frequency |
|------|--------------|----------|-----------|
| Diltiazem CD/SR | 120–180 mg | 360 mg | Daily (CD) |
| Verapamil SR | 180 mg | 480 mg | 1–2× daily |

## Common Side Effects

| DHP CCB | Non-DHP CCB |
|---------|-------------|
| Ankle edema (dose-dependent) | Constipation (verapamil) |
| Headache, flushing | Bradycardia, heart block |
| Dizziness | Gingival hyperplasia |
| Reflex tachycardia (short-acting avoided) | Negative inotropy (caution in HFrEF) |

## Contraindications & Cautions

| CCB | Contraindications / Cautions |
|-----|------------------------------|
| All CCBs | Avoid short-acting nifedipine (risk of reflex tachycardia, adverse outcomes) |
| Verapamil, Diltiazem | Heart block, severe LV dysfunction (HFrEF), sick sinus syndrome (without pacemaker) |
| Verapamil | Avoid with beta-blockers (additive bradycardia) |
